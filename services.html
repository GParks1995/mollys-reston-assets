Services

Comprehensive care tailored to your dog’s needs

Daycare

Structured playgroups, enrichment and socialisation

Play & Learn

Dogs are grouped by size and temperament to ensure safe and engaging play. Our certified handlers facilitate games and activities that build confidence and social skills.

Enrichment

Mental stimulation is just as important as physical exercise. We include scent work, puzzle toys and agility courses that challenge your dog’s mind and keep boredom at bay.

Safety First

We maintain strict staff‑to‑dog ratios and employ industry‑leading sanitisation practices. All play is monitored and we enforce vaccination and health protocols for every guest.

Boarding

Comfortable stays that feel like home

Private Suites

Our climate‑controlled suites provide cosy beds, soft lighting and plenty of space to stretch out. We customise each stay based on your dog’s routine and preferences.

Webcam Access

Check in on your pet anytime. Our secure webcams allow you to see your dog enjoying their stay, bringing you peace of mind wherever you are.

Bedtime Routine

Dogs enjoy nightly tuck‑ins, calming music and bedtime treats. We strive to mirror the comforts of home and help them settle in for a restful night.

Grooming & Spa

Keep your dog looking and feeling their best

Bath & Brush

A refreshing bath, premium conditioning and a thorough brush‑out leave coats soft and shiny. Includes nail trim and ear cleaning.

Full Groom

Breed‑specific cuts or personalised styles performed by our professional groomers. We listen to your requests and make sure your pup looks fabulous.

Spa Upgrades

Indulge your dog with deshedding treatments, paw balms, blueberry facials and more. Our spa menu provides a luxurious experience for even the most discerning pups.
