Frequently Asked Questions

What vaccines are required?

We require proof of Rabies, Bordetella and Distemper vaccinations. Dogs must wait 48 hours after receiving vaccinations before any service.

Can I tour the facility?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective clients to schedule a tour. Please call ahead to arrange a time and ensure the safety of our current guests.

Do you accept large breeds?

Dogs over 80lbs are accepted on a case‑by‑case basis, provided they are regulars and comfortable in our environment. Please contact us to discuss your dog’s needs.
