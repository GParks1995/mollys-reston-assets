Step-by-Step Grooming Booking Process
1. Greet client & ask dog’s name, breed, and service needs 2. Check facility calendar for open slots 3. Use pricing sheet to quote (“Starts at $X…final price after assessment”) 4. Confirm vaccine status (Rabies, Bordetella, Distemper) 5. Add to Gingr & leave your initials in notes 6. Ask about add-ons/spa 7. Confirm with groomer for big dogs or special requests 8. Never guarantee slots unless booking now 9. Review tip, loyalty, & review programs at checkout
Key Front Desk & Staff Policies:
  • Breed-Based Pricing: Always quote the more expensive breed if mixed. Never promise a final price—always “starts at” language.
  • Vaccine Policy: Rabies, Bordetella, Distemper required. 48-hr wait post-vaccination before any service.
  • No Waitlist in Gingr: Use “Call List” for unavailable slots. Never add to waitlist.
  • Weight Policy: Dogs 80+ lbs accepted case-by-case, only if regulars & groomer approves. Direct owner to email groomer.
  • Escalation: Unsure? Never say “I don’t know”—always offer to check with groomer or manager and follow up.
  • Overbooking/Scheduling: Always check calendar for time & weight limits before accepting requests.

What’s Included in Each Service

  • Bath & Brush: Double shampoo & conditioner, tearless facial, paw/sanitary clean, blow dry, brush-out (5 min undercoat removal), paw pad shave, ear clean, nail cut.
  • Bath & Tidy: All above + light body/face trim of owner’s choice, silhouette trim, sanitary clip.
  • Cut & Style: All above + full haircut (per preference), extended brush-out (15 min undercoat), paw pad shave, sanitary clip.
  • Any other service is an add-on or a la carte! See below.

Molly’s Pro Tips (Staff!):
  • Always confirm final price with groomer before client checks out—never rely only on sheet if there are any doubts.
  • Leave your initials in Gingr appointment notes for follow-up and accountability.
  • Encourage clients to pre-book (“Book your next now for a discount!”) and ask for reviews (“You get 10% off with a Google or Facebook review!”).
  • If you don’t know an answer: “Our groomer is the best person to answer that! Let me check and get back to you.”
  • For 80+ lb or reactive dogs, politely direct owner to groomer’s email.

Client FAQ

Q: How do I know what service my dog needs?
A: We’ll help you choose! Our team looks at your pup’s coat, preferences, and lifestyle to recommend the perfect service.
Q: What’s included in a grooming appointment?
A: Every service includes double shampoo, conditioner, tearless facial, paw/sanitary clean, blow dry, brush out, ear clean, and nail trim. Add-ons available!
Q: How much will it cost?
A: Pricing is by breed and coat. We’ll give you an estimate when you book and confirm the final price at check-in after seeing your dog.
Q: Can I add special treatments?
A: Absolutely! We offer paw balms, de-shedding, puppy spa, dental care, and more. Just ask!
Q: What vaccines are required?
A: Rabies, Bordetella, Distemper (48-hour wait after vaccine for any service).
Q: Can you groom large breeds?
A: We take dogs over 80 lbs on a case-by-case basis, if they are regular clients and comfortable with grooming. Please email our groomer for details.
Q: How do I tip?
A: Tip by card, Venmo/PayPal QR, or cash. We appreciate it!
Q: How do I rebook or earn discounts?
A: Leave us a review and get 10% off your next groom! Pre-book five appointments and your sixth is 25% off.
Q: What if I have a special request or my dog has sensitivities?
A: Let us know—our groomer will customize products and handling to suit your pup’s needs.
Escalation & Client Communication:
  • If you cannot answer a question: “Our groomer can best answer that. May I have your info for a callback?” Log on the Call List, fill out all client/pet info for follow-up.
  • Never bring a dog back unless groomer asks you to.
  • If client requests groomer’s number, tell them to text (not call), include their name, dog’s name, breed, and what service they want.
  • Push “Farewell Freshen-up” bath for boarding clients before pickup.
