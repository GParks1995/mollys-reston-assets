Key Front Desk & Staff Policies

  • Breed-Based Pricing: Always quote the more expensive breed if mixed. Never promise a final price—always “starts at” language.
  • Vaccine Policy: Rabies, Bordetella, Distemper required. 48-hour wait post-vaccination before any service.
  • No Waitlist in Gingr: Use “Call List” for unavailable slots. Never add to waitlist.
  • Weight Policy: Dogs 80+ lbs accepted case-by-case, only if regulars & groomer approves. Direct owner to email groomer.
  • Escalation: Unsure? Never say “I don’t know”—always offer to check with groomer or manager and follow up.
  • Overbooking/Scheduling: Always check calendar for time & weight limits before accepting requests.

Molly’s Pro Tips (Staff!)

  • Always confirm final price with groomer before client checks out—never rely only on sheet if there are any doubts.
  • Leave your initials in Gingr appointment notes for follow‑up and accountability.
  • Encourage clients to pre‑book (“Book your next now for a discount!”) and ask for reviews (“You get 10% off with a Google or Facebook review!”).
  • If you don’t know an answer: “Our groomer is the best person to answer that! Let me check and get back to you.”
  • For 80+ lb or reactive dogs, politely direct owner to groomer’s email.

Client FAQ

How do I know what service my dog needs?

We’ll help you choose! Our team looks at your pup’s coat, preferences, and lifestyle to recommend the perfect service.

What’s included in a grooming appointment?

Every service includes double shampoo, conditioner, tearless facial, paw/sanitary clean, blow dry, brush out, ear clean, and nail trim. Add‑ons available!

How much will it cost?

Pricing is by breed and coat. We’ll give you an estimate when you book and confirm the final price at check‑in after seeing your dog.

Can I add special treatments?

Absolutely! We offer paw balms, de‑shedding, puppy spa, dental care, and more. Just ask!

What vaccines are required?

Rabies, Bordetella, Distemper (48‑hour wait after vaccine for any service).

Can you groom large breeds?

We take dogs over 80 lbs on a case‑by‑case basis, if they are regular clients and comfortable with grooming. Please email our groomer for details.

How do I tip?

Tip by card, Venmo/PayPal QR, or cash. We appreciate it!

How do I rebook or earn discounts?

Leave us a review and get 10% off your next groom! Pre‑book five appointments and your sixth is 25% off.

What if I have a special request or my dog has sensitivities?

Let us know—our groomer will customize products and handling to suit your pup’s needs.

Escalation & Client Communication

  • If you cannot answer a question: “Our groomer can best answer that. May I have your info for a callback?” Log on the Call List, fill out all client/pet info for follow‑up.
  • Never bring a dog back unless groomer asks you to.
  • If client requests groomer’s number, tell them to text (not call), include their name, dog’s name, breed, and what service they want.
  • Push “Farewell Freshen‑up” bath for boarding clients before pickup.
