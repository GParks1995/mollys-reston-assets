Our Services

Daycare

Our daycare program offers structured playgroups tailored to your dog’s size and temperament. Certified staff supervise every session ensuring safety while your pup socialises, exercises and enjoys brain games. Enrichment activities like scent trails, puzzle toys and agility courses keep minds sharp and tails wagging.

Boarding

Your dog’s home away from home. Our boarding suites provide comfortable bedding, calming music and nightly tuck‑ins. We maintain your pup’s routine with regular meals, potty breaks and plenty of snuggles. Ask about our webcam access to check in on your furry family while you’re away.

Grooming

Our salon team uses gentle techniques and premium products to pamper your pet. Every service includes a double shampoo and conditioning, blow dry, brush‑out, nail trim and ear clean. Upgrades range from deshedding treatments to blueberry facials. We tailor each groom to your dog’s breed and lifestyle.
